Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Versatile Prime Infosoft Private Limited ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application, Doodhiya (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you log in via Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture URL.
  • User-Generated Content: If you upload a profile image, it is processed and stored securely via Cloudinary.
  • Technical Data: We collect basic device information (OS version, model) via Firebase to help fix bugs and improve performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Authenticating your identity and providing access to your account.
  • Personalizing your user experience.
  • Maintaining and improving the App's stability.
  • Protecting against fraud or unauthorized access.

3. Third-Party Services

For the App to function properly, we work with the following third-party providers:

  • Google Play Services / Firebase: Used for authentication and crash reporting.
  • Cloudinary: Used for secure image hosting and delivery.
  • Google Sign-In: To facilitate secure social authentication.

4. Data Deletion & Account Closure

We respect your right to be forgotten. Users can request the deletion of their personal data and account by:

  • Contacting our support team at support@versatileitsolution.com.
  • Once a request is received, we will verify your identity and delete all associated records from Firebase and Cloudinary within 7 business days.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption, to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
